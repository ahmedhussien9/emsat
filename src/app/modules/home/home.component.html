The Ministry of Education

The Ministry of Education Booking Exams App, is a unified management platform that helps securely store and access test administration and candidate data, allow candidates to self-register for exams, allow test centers to view and manage their information, and provide basic descriptive statistics on stored data.

Exam and Test Centers:

Through this platform test centers will be able to manage the test center information regarding test monitoring as well as follow up on materials provided by the EmSAT unit, help support during tests in logging candidates attendance and report any incident.

  • Manage the Test Center attributes
  • View upcoming test dates/committees test dates
  • Commit to participate in specific test dates
  • Manage registered candidates (View registered candidates and generate related reports and lists)
  • View/Download Emsat Administration materials
  • Record candidates attendance details
  • Raise incident in Service desk

Candidates and Applicants:

Through the platform candidates can manage their test administration by viewing available dates and registering for the preferable ones as well as viewing all of their information related to test taking.

  • Register for Emsat
  • Check test calendar and upcoming test registration
  • Book and Pay for tests (cancel/change test booking)
  • Raise incident in Service desk
  • Request specific Needs accommodation
